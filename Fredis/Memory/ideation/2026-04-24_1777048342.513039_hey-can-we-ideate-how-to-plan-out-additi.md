**Pipeline**
1. Ideation happens in one place — #ideation thread, DM, or voice-memo. Fredis listens but doesn't act until the thread is marked "done" (emoji react or `/fanout`).
2. On trigger, Fredis classifies the conversation into discrete "seeds", each tagged to one channel (engineering, product, security, content, CRM, …).
3. Per seed → a draft file at `Fredis/Memory/drafts/active/ideation/<date>_<slug>.md` with the originating context, a suggested implementation plan, and the target channel.
4. Per seed → a `[DRAFT-IDEA]` notice posted to that channel linking the draft, plus a HubSpot ticket in an "Ideation" pipeline (reuses the existing Review-queue stages Drafted → In review → Needs send → Actioned/Rejected).

**Tradeoffs worth deciding before building**
- **Granularity** — one ticket per seed could mean 20 tickets from a long brainstorm. Mitigation: two-pass. Fredis first posts a *theme index* in #ideation; you 👍 the ones worth fanning out.
- **Classification accuracy** — "auth hardening" could land in #product or #security. Mitigation: Fredis shows top-2 channels with confidence and you confirm with a reaction.
- **Trigger model** — automatic watch vs explicit command. Auto feels magical but spams on half-finished thinking. Explicit `/fanout` matches advisor-mode (nothing leaves ideation until you say so).
