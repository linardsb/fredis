Wanted you to have a look at the AI-in-agriculture space — it's where I've been putting most of my reading time this year and I think you'd find it interesting. Below is the shortlist I've been working from. You don't need to read all of it, but even skimming two or three of these gives a pretty clear map of where the sector is heading.

**Start here (cross-cutting):**
- **AgFunder Network** — weekly newsletter, best single source on where money is flowing in agtech
- ***AgTech…So What?*** — Sarah Nolet's podcast, operator/investor conversations
- ***Future of Agriculture*** — Tim Hammerich's podcast, more farmer-voice
- **Rob Leclerc on X** — one of the sharper VC voices in the space

**UK angle:**
- **DEFRA Future Farming blog** — policy direction, SFI/ELM scheme updates
- **AHDB** (Agriculture & Horticulture Development Board) — sector reports and market data, free
- **Farmers Weekly** — trade publication, ground-level view

**Latvia / EU angle:**
- **LLKC** (Latvijas Lauku konsultāciju un izglītības centrs) — consulting centre, sector view
- **Zemnieku saeima (ZSA)** — farmers' union, policy pulse
- **CAP Strategic Plan Latvia** — on the EC site, if you want the policy skeleton
