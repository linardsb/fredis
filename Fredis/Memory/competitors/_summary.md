## Master rules (derived from the landscape)

1. **Don't compete on hours in Argentina.** Always methodology + cross-jurisdiction + EU/UK credibility.
2. **Don't compete on local content in Sussex yet.** Artemis + Darcy have a multi-year head start; find a vertical angle instead.
3. **Don't compete on language with Tilde.** Partner or avoid — never compete.
4. **Do compete on productised methodology.** Nobody in my tier (UK / LV / AR) has productised UK↔LV↔AR agri-AI. Moat opportunity.
5. **Do compete on timing niches.** AEO is 12-month window in UK; AI-in-mid-market-ag is 18–24 months in Baltic. Faster decision beats better analysis.
6. **Treat AgriMind, AUGA, Bioceres, LBTU, LLKC, CREA, INTA, MILA, EIT Food as partners first.** Competition framing costs me access; partnership framing gets me introductions.
